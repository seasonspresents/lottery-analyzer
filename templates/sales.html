Rule #1
Buy Consecutive Tickets
Always buy multiple tickets from the same roll. Prize distribution across a roll means consecutive tickets dramatically increase your chance of hitting a winner.
Rule #2
Never Switch Mid-Session
Stick to one game per store visit. Jumping between games resets your probability window and eliminates the consecutive-ticket advantage.
Rule #3

When you win, bank half immediately. Reinvest the other half into the same game, same roll. If you lose the reinvestment round, stop. Don't chase.
